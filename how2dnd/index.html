How 2 D&D

Welcome to How 2 D&D! Our goal here is to teach you the basics of how to play the TTRPG Dungeons and Dragons, either as a Player or as a Dungeon Master! We'll teach you everything from character creation to worldbuilding, and give you some tips on how to make each play session worth the time you spend.

Players

If you're here to learn how to play the game, this is for you! We'll teach you how to build a character, how to play as your character, and how to interact with the world and the other players at the table.

Dungeon Masters

If you're here to learn the secrets of running a campaign, we'll teach you how to make a world (or use a pre-made one if that's more your style), add smaller details to that world, and work with your friends to make your game night one to remember!
